5 Vulnerability Management

Q: How do you prioritize vulnerabilities?

"Not just CVSS. I factor in EPSS (Exploit Prediction Scoring System), asset criticality, and compensating controls."

Prioritization Framework:

CVSS: Tells you severity (how bad IF exploited)

EPSS: Tells you urgency (likelihood of exploitation in next 30 days)

Asset Context: Tells you impact (crown jewel vs test server)

Compensating Controls: Network segmentation, WAF, EDR coverage that reduce risk

TVM + Qualys Integration:

TVM: Microsoft ecosystem, real-time, integrated with MDE, software inventory, security recommendations

Qualys VMDR: Broader coverage (Linux, network devices, non-Microsoft), scheduled scans, compliance reporting

Both feed into Sentinel for unified visibility and correlation with threat detections

"CVSS tells you severity. EPSS tells you urgency. Asset context tells you impact."
